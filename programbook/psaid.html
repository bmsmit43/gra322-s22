GRA 362 Visual Communication IV

The Public Service Announcements for International Disasters (PSAid) contest was developed to challenge college students to produce public service materials that creatively articulate why “Cash is Best” when helping people affected by emergencies overseas. Cash contributions allow relief organizations to purchase exactly what is most urgently needed to help survivors.
