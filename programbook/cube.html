Cube

GRA 220 Design Drawing

Set plane model on the desk at arms length to the right of the computer if right-handed and to the left if left-handed. As a reference for the instructor, photograph the plane model as it appears to the student from their eye level. Then draw the plane model in Adobe Illustrator as it appears to the student from their eye level. Once the plane drawing is proportional to the plane model replace the plane model with the cube model, photograph, and extend the plane drawing into a cube.
